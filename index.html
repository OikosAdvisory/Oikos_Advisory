Who We Are

Rooted in an Ancient Ideal

"The oikos was not merely a house — it was a living covenant of care, continuity, and collective protection."

Oikos Advisory is a dedicated insurance branch under the CASB Agency, specialising in life insurance — with general insurance on the horizon. We take our name from the ancient Greek concept of the household: the original unit of protection, provision, and legacy.

We believe that insurance is not a product; it is a promise — a shield forged for the people you love and the life you have built.
